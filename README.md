## Projects

### HR Workforce Attrition Analysis Dashboard

**Tech Stack:** Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel

**Project link:** https://github.com/PRATHAMPBHANDARY/HR-Workforce-Attrition-Analysis-Dashboard-


**Overview**
Developed an interactive HR analytics dashboard to analyze employee attrition patterns and identify key factors impacting workforce retention.

**Key Highlights**

* Designed dashboard to analyze attrition across departments, job roles, and demographics
* Tracked key metrics including attrition rate (16%), total employees (1480), and workforce distribution
* Identified key drivers such as salary range, age group, and job satisfaction
* Performed data cleaning using Power Query and created KPIs using DAX

**Key Insights**

* Highest attrition observed among employees aged 26–35
* Majority attrition occurs in lower salary bands (≤ 5K)
* R&D and Sales departments show higher attrition trends
* Compensation and job satisfaction significantly influence retention

**Business Impact**

* Enables data-driven HR decision-making
* Helps identify and reduce employee attrition
* Supports development of effective retention strategies
